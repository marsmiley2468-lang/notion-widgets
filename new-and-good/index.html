Kairos 30.2: AI-Generated Games Interrogate Generative AI in the Humanities
The new Spring 2026 issue of Kairos features Anastasia Salter's 'AI Admin: Provocations through Generated Play,' which uses generative AI to build two playable games — one simulating the pressures of academic administration around AI policy, the other centered on digital humanities. The piece models multimodal composition as critical practice, making it directly relevant to writing pedagogy and AI ethics in the classroom.

French Polynesia Expands Marine Protection to 1.4 Million Square Kilometers
On June 7, 2026, French Polynesia's president announced a 520,000-square-kilometer expansion of fully protected ocean waters, bringing the country's total marine protected zone to 1.4 million sq km. The expanded sanctuary will safeguard 20 shark species including the critically endangered scalloped hammerhead, along with hundreds of mollusk and open-ocean fish species.
