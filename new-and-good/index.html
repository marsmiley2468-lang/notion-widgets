The World's Largest Wildlife Overpass Just Got Its First Elk Visitors
Colorado's Greenland Wildlife Overpass — currently the largest in the world — recorded its first three elk crossings six months after opening. Designed specifically for elk, mule deer, and pronghorn (notoriously wary of underpasses), the bridge was completed ahead of schedule and on budget. Wildlife officials called the crossing "proof the new structure is working."
