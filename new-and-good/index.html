French Polynesia Expands Protected Ocean Waters by 520,000 Square Kilometers
France announced a massive expansion of protected waters near French Polynesian islands, bringing total protected waters to 1.4 million square kilometers. The new zone will safeguard 20 shark species (including the critically endangered scalloped hammerhead), 455 mollusk species, and 60 open-ocean fish species.
