Green Sea Turtles Downlisted From Endangered — A Rare Conservation Win
Green sea turtles were moved off the endangered species list by the IUCN in 2025, marking one of marine biology's clearest recent success stories. Decades of beach protections, fishing gear regulations, and international cooperation drove the population recovery. It's a concrete reminder that sustained, coordinated conservation effort actually works.
